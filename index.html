About

Neon Thunder

Neon Thunder is a glam rock band built on the premise that the 80s got it right — big riffs, bigger hair, and songs that hit you like a freight train doing 90 in a rainstorm.

Equal parts Mötley Crüe swagger and Poison hooks, they make music that doesn't flinch, doesn't apologize, and doesn't turn the volume down. Released through Hello Texas Records, their debut album Electric Nights is a full-throttle statement of intent.

This isn't nostalgia. This is how rock and roll was always supposed to sound.
